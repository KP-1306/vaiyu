A Note on Strategy

Classic performs better for value-driven shoppers (typical artificial-jewelry buyer at ₹500–1,500 price point). Premium works better when you want to charge ₹1,500–5,000+ and build a brand that justifies it. The architecture beneath both is identical — you can switch the visual layer later without rebuilding.
