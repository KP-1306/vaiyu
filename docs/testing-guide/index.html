What is VAiyu?

VAiyu is an all-in-one operations system for hotels — built for small and mid-sized properties in India (with a focus on Uttarakhand: Dehradun, Rishikesh, the Char Dham route). One login runs the entire hotel: room bookings and walk-in check-ins, guest folios and payments (cash, UPI, card, Razorpay), housekeeping, food orders, guest service requests, staff shifts, room pricing, finance, and a "Growth Hub" of marketing tools (leads, packages, local SEO, OTA readiness). Everything updates in real time.

🏠
Runs the hotel

Owner / Manager

Uses the Owner Console — dashboard, money, staff, pricing, marketing. The back office and command centre. This is most of this guide.

🛎️
Works the floor

Front-desk & Staff

Use the Ops Board, Kitchen screen and Staff App to handle check-ins, service requests, and food orders as they come in.

📱
Staying at the hotel

Guest

Uses the Guest Portal on their phone (via a room QR code) to see their stay, order food, request service, view the bill, and check out.

How the three connect — one example
A guest taps "extra towels" in the Guest Portal → it appears instantly on the staff Ops Board with an SLA timer → a housekeeper marks it done → the owner sees the response time on the Dashboard. Money flows the same way: a walk-in check-in creates a folio → payments post to it → the Dashboard shows revenue and the outstanding balance owed. Everything you do on one screen shows up on the others in seconds.

How a new user opens VAiyu and signs in

STEP 01

Open VAiyu & Sign In

/ → /signin

VAiyu opens on a marketing landing page. Hotel staff sign in with a passwordless magic link — you enter your email, get a one-time link, click it, and land on your hotel's Owner Console. There is no password to remember.

  • 1 Open the app URL your VAiyu admin shared (here: http://localhost:8080). You'll see the landing page below.
  • 2 Click Sign in (top-right) to reach /signin.
  • 3 Enter the email your hotel was registered with and click "Send magic link".
  • 4 Open the email (check spam too) and click the link — it expires in 10 minutes.
  • 5 You're redirected to your Owner Console at /owner/<your-hotel>.
✅ After the link, you land on the Dashboard showing "Good morning/afternoon/evening, <Hotel Name>".
ℹ️ In this test build the magic-link step is skipped — a dev auto-login signs you in instantly, so visiting /owner/dev-hotel takes you straight to the Dashboard. The screenshot below is the real public landing page (the entry point).

Front Desk — Walk-in check-in

/checkin → Instant Residency

For guests who arrive without a booking. The Front Desk screen offers two paths: Digital Arrival (look up an existing reservation) and Instant Residency (a brand-new walk-in). Instant Residency opens a "Guest Registration" form, then room selection and payment. GST is applied automatically from hotel settings.

  • 1 Click Front Desk in the menu (or "Walk-in check-in" on the Dashboard). You'll see the two arrival cards.

  • 6 Confirm. The new guest then appears in Arrivals as "Checked In".
✅ The registration form accepts input, tax is added automatically, and the booking + folio are created on confirm.

STEP 07

Revenue, Folio & Money

Arrivals → Collect

A "folio" is a guest's running bill. Money in VAiyu lives in three places: the Dashboard cards (Revenue / ADR / Outstanding), each guest's folio (room charges, tax, payments, balance), and Payments & Settlements (Step 12, for Razorpay payouts). The cleanest view of the money math is the folio drawer, opened from Arrivals.

  • 1 Go to Arrivals and click Collect on Asha Verma. A drawer opens with Summary / Folio / Payments / Activity tabs.
  • 2 On the Folio tab, read the math: Room Charges ₹3,125 + Tax ₹375 = ₹3,500, minus Payments ₹2,000 = Outstanding ₹1,500.
  • 3 Click Collect Payment to record a cash/UPI/card payment against the balance. (Payments are immutable once recorded — refunds are added as new entries, never edits.)
  • 4 Cross-check: the Dashboard's Outstanding ₹5,000 = Asha ₹1,500 + Rohan ₹3,500. The numbers reconcile across screens.
✅ Folio totals add up exactly (charges − payments = outstanding) and match the Dashboard's outstanding figure.
ℹ️ The standalone /revenue page and the ADR/RevPAR trend charts populate only after several days of real bookings — on a brand-new hotel they're intentionally sparse. Today's money is on the Dashboard + folios.
  Folio drawer — Room ₹3,125 + Tax ₹375 − ₹2,000 paid = ₹1,500 due

STEP 10

Dynamic Pricing

/owner/dev-hotel/pricing

Adjust room rates by occupancy, date, or day-of-week. A key safety idea: the engine runs in Recommend-only mode (suggests prices, never writes them) until you switch it to Auto-apply. Guardrails cap how far a price or a walk-in discount can swing.

  • 1 Click Dynamic Pricing in the menu. Top cards show Occupancy 50% (2/4), Inventory 4 (2 available).
  • 2 Note the Recommend-only vs Auto-apply toggle and the Max price-swing guardrail (25%).
  • 3 In Price Evaluation, enter a base price (e.g. 2000) and a stay date, click Evaluate to see the recommended rate.
  • 4 Open Manage Rules to add a rule (e.g. weekend +20%), Rate Calendar for per-date rates, and History for past changes.
✅ Evaluate returns a recommendation. A new rule saves and shows in the Rate Calendar for its date range.
  Dynamic Pricing — occupancy, engine mode, guardrails, price evaluation

Payments & Settlements

/owner/dev-hotel/payments

The money ledger for online (Razorpay) payments — what was collected, refunded, the net to your hotel, and the platform fee. Cash/UPI collected at the desk lives on the folio (Step 07); this screen is specifically the online-settlement view, with payout timing (T+1 to T+3) governed by your Razorpay agreement.

  • 1 Click Payments & Settlements under "Finance" in the menu.

  • 3 Use the range tabs (Today / 7 days / 30 days / Month / All time), method and status filters.
  • 4 Reconcile and Export CSV help match payouts against your bank statement.
○ Empty state on purpose: the demo's payment is a cash collection (on the folio), so this Razorpay-only ledger shows ₹0 / "No payments in this window". Take a Razorpay payment to see rows here.
